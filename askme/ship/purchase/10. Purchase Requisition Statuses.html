Purchase Requisition Statuses

Step-by-Step Instructions

Filtering by transaction status

Step 1:

  • Click on the ‘Requisition’ tab and select the desired status from options like Draft, Awaiting Approval, Approved, Rejected, Under Processing, Canceled, Ordered, Partially Received, Closed, or All.



Step 2:

  • Selecting ‘All’ will display Purchase requisition of every status.




Searching using Free Text

Step 1:

  • Go to ‘Requisition’ and select ‘All’ or any other transaction status.





Step 2:

  • Click on the search bar and type in your search term, such as the description of the PR.



Step 3:

  • The search results will display matching Purchase requisition.



Applying Multiple Filters

Step 1:

  • Go to ‘Requisition’ and select ‘All’.



Step 2:

  • Click on the Filter button to open Filter Pane and click on - Filter button, select transaction Status.




Step 3:

  • Select Receipt Status as Awaiting Approval. All Awaiting Approval PRs would be listed.



Step 4:

  • Again, click on - the Filter button and select Order Priority. Select Order Priority as Defect.





Step 5:

  • All PRs with Transaction Status (Awaiting Approval) and Order Priority (Defect) will be listed.
