Purchase Requisition cancellation

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1:

  • Go to Requisition>>All (Approved) to open the requisitions listing screen.



Step 2:

  • Select the Purchase Requisition to be cancelled.
    Go to Process>>Request For Cancellation.



Step 3:

  • Click on Yes.



Step 4:

  • Enter reason for cancellation request and click on Ok.



Step 5:

  • Cancellation request sent. Status can be tracked in the Fact box.
