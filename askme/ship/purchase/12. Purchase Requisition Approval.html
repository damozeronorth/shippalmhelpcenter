Purchase Requisition Approval

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1:

  • From the Home Page, click on the cue card for Requisitions to approve.



Step 2:

  • From the listing page, select the Purchase Requisition & click on “Approve”.



Step 3:

  • The approver has the option to review & change the Need Date if required before approval. Earlier, the approver would send reject and send back to the originator for updating the Need Date. This improves the workflow and improve productivity.



Step 4:

  • Update the Need Date based on need.



Step 5:

  • Click “Yes” in the prompt message.



Step 6:

  • Need Date updated.



Step 7:

  • Now return to the Listing screen. Click Approve to approve the Purchase Requisition.
