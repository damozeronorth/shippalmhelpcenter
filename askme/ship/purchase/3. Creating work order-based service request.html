Creating work order-based service request

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1:

  • Go to Work Orders>> All.



Step 2:

  • Open the work order, under Lines, click on Dropdown>> Create Service PR.



    • Step 3:

    • Requisition created successfully. Under General tab, update Description, Type, Category, Priority, Need date & Order Type.



    Step 4:

    • Once all the items are included, click on Request Approval>> Send Approval Request.
