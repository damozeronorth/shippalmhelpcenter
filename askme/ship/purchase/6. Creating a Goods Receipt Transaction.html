Creating a Goods Receipt Transaction

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1:

  • From the home page, go to “Purchase order”, then click “ALL”.



Step 2:

  • Search for the PO and open it.



Step 3:

  • Make sure the transaction status of the Purchase Order as "Ordered" and the receipt status as "Not Received".



Step 4:

  • Under Lines, select the items by ticking the checkbox for which the receipt must be made.



Step 5:

  • Update the Received Quantity under quantity To Receive section.



Step 6:

  • From the Posting tab, click on the Post button.



Step 7:

  • Now goods Receipt note is generated.The Purchase orders transaction status changes to closed and fully received or Ordered and Partially received.
