Stock Adjustment Transaction

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1:

  • From the Role Centre, click on Actions and select Physical Inventory Journal.



Step 2:

  • Now, Click on Prepare and select Calculate Inventory.



Step 3:

  • Update the document number.
    Select the location for which the Inventory will be checked and click OK.



Step 4:

  • All items from the location will be displayed. Update the quantity as determined from the physical count under QTY. (Physical. Inventory).
    Then select the reason code.



Step 5:

  • Once you’ve updated the quantities, go to ‘Posting’ and click ‘Post’.



Step 6:

  • The application will automatically update the Remaining on Board (ROB) based on the batch.
