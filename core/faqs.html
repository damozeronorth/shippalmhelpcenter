Frequently Asked Questions

Tag No.: PUR-001 How do I create a new purchase order?

Navigate to the Purchase module, select “New Order,” and fill in the required fields.

Tag No.: PUR-002 How can I cancel a purchase order?

Go to the Purchase module, find the order, and select “Cancel Order.”

Tag No.: PUR-003 What documents are required for a purchase order?

Required documents include invoices, receipts, and approval forms.

Tag No.: PUR-004 Can I edit a submitted purchase order?

Only pending orders can be edited. Go to “Edit Order” in the Purchase module.

Tag No.: PUR-005 How do I view my purchase order history?

Go to “Order History” in the Purchase module to view all orders.

Tag No.: VOY-001 How do I add a new voyage?

In the Voyage module, select “Add New Voyage” and fill in the necessary details.

Tag No.: VOY-002 How do I update voyage details?

In the Voyage module, select the voyage you wish to update and click “Edit.” Make the necessary changes and save your updates.

Tag No.: VOY-003 Can I delete a voyage?

Yes, to delete a voyage, locate the voyage in the Voyage module, select it, and click “Delete.” Note that this action may be irreversible.

Tag No.: VOY-004 How do I view voyage history?

To view voyage history, go to the Voyage module, select the “History” tab, and browse through previous voyages.

Tag No.: VOY-005 How do I set voyage alerts?

In the Voyage module, go to “Settings” and select “Alerts.” You can configure notifications for voyage-related events here.

Tag No.: PMS-001 What is PMS maintenance?

PMS maintenance is the regular upkeep of machinery and systems.

Tag No.: PMS-002 How do I schedule a new maintenance task?

In the PMS module, select “Add New Task,” enter the details, and set a scheduled date to create a new maintenance task.

Tag No.: PMS-003 How can I view the status of ongoing maintenance tasks?

To view task statuses, go to the PMS module’s “Task List” and filter by status to see ongoing, completed, or overdue tasks.

Tag No.: PMS-004 Can I defer a maintenance task?

Yes, select the task in the PMS module, click “Defer,” and choose a new date. Remember to provide a reason for deferral if required.

Tag No.: PMS-005 How do I mark a maintenance task as complete?

Open the task in the PMS module, select “Complete,” add any relevant notes, and save to mark the task as done.

Tag No.: PMS-006 How do I generate a maintenance report?

Go to the “Reports” section in the PMS module, choose the desired time frame and task type, and click “Generate Report.”

Tag No.: CER-001 How do I renew a certificate?

Navigate to the Certificate module, select the certificate, and choose “Renew.”

Tag No.: CER-002 How do I upload a new certificate?

In the Certificate module, click “Add New Certificate,” upload the document, and fill in the required details.

Tag No.: CER-003 Can I delete an expired certificate?

Yes, to delete an expired certificate, locate it in the Certificate module, select it, and click “Delete.” Be cautious, as this action may be permanent.

Tag No.: CER-004 How do I view certificate expiration dates?

To check expiration dates, open the Certificate module and select the “Expiry Dates” view to see upcoming and past expirations.

Tag No.: CER-005 How do I update certificate details?

In the Certificate module, select the certificate you want to update, click “Edit,” make necessary changes, and save.

Tag No.: CER-006 How do I set up certificate renewal reminders?

Go to the Certificate module’s “Settings” section and configure renewal reminders based on your preferred timeframe.

Tag No.: HSEQ-001What is HSEQ compliance?

HSEQ compliance ensures health, safety, environment, and quality standards.

Tag No.: HSEQ-002 How do I report a safety incident?

To report a safety incident, go to the HSEQ module, select “Report Incident,” fill in the incident details, and submit the report for review.

Tag No.: HSEQ-003 How can I view environmental compliance records?

In the HSEQ module, navigate to the “Environmental Compliance” section to access past and current compliance records.

Tag No.: HSEQ-004 How do I submit a quality assurance checklist?

Go to the HSEQ module, select “Quality Assurance,” choose “Submit Checklist,” and complete the required fields to submit.

Tag No.: HSEQ-005 Can I schedule a safety audit?

Yes, to schedule a safety audit, open the HSEQ module, go to “Safety Audits,” select “Schedule Audit,” and set the preferred date and details.

Tag No.: HSEQ-006 How do I update corrective actions from an audit?

In the HSEQ module, access the relevant audit report, select “Corrective Actions,” make the updates, and save the changes.
