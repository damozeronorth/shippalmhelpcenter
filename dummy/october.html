2.Features

2.1 Module-Purchase

1.Cue cards addition in ShipPalm home page (TFS-160711)

To provide Quick access to Purchase quotes submitted by vendors, a cue card has been introduced for Purchase quotes. This functionality is available for the users having Purchaser role at the shore side On clicking the cue card, the system will list all purchase quotes submitted by the vendors (In approved Status) This will help organisations to make decision at the right time and reduce the need for internal/external follow-ups.

Reproduction steps:

  • Log on to ShipPalm
  • Scroll down to Purchaser Activities
  • Look for Cue card “Purchase Quote” and click
  • Screen will display list Purchase quotes for various vendors
  • Search /filter option can be used to look for specific Purchase quote

2.2. Item selection in Purchase Documents (TFS-191069)

To improve the usability, the system will list only those items that are mapped to the vessel for which the purchase document is being created. This will significantly reduce the time taken to create or modify Purchase Requisition and Vessel Purchase order Currently the system lists out all the items at the Business unit level while creating a Purchase Requisition (PR) or Direct Purchase Orders. User needs to know the item that belongs to the vessel. This is time consuming one and at times may end up in wrong item selection

Reproduction steps:

  1. Log on to ShipPalm with Purchaser credentials
  2. On the Home page, Select Purchase Requisition and click on ALL
  3. From the listing page, click New. Now a Purchase Requisition screens opens
  4. Click on three dots to select the required vessel from No. Series
  5. Fill up the required as well as mandatory fields
  6. Go to Line section and Click in three dots (…) Item selection
