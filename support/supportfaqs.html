Support FAQs

Tag No.: SUP-1.1 What is a support ticket, and when should I submit one?

A support ticket is a formal request for assistance. You should submit one if you need help with technical issues, account support, or if you have general questions or feedback.

Tag No.: SUP-1.2 How do I submit a support ticket?

Visit our support portal, click "Submit a Ticket," fill out the form with relevant details, and select the appropriate category.”

Tag No.: SUP-2.1 What ticket categories should I choose from?

Categories include Technical Issue, Account Support, Feature Request, and General Inquiry. Select the one that best matches your request.

Tag No.: SUP-2.2 What if I’m unsure which category my issue falls under?

If unsure, select “General Inquiry,” and our support team will direct it to the appropriate department.

Tag No.: SUP-3.1 How can I check the status of my submitted ticket?

You can log in to the support portal, go to “My Tickets,” and view the status updates on your ticket.

Tag No.: SUP-3.2 What do the different ticket statuses mean?

New: Your ticket has been submitted and is awaiting review.
In Progress: A support agent is actively working on your issue.
Waiting for Customer: The support team needs more information from you to proceed.
Resolved: Your issue has been addressed and the ticket is closed.

Tag No.: SUP-4.1 What are the typical response times for support tickets?

Response times vary based on ticket priority. Urgent issues are addressed within 1-2 hours, while lower-priority tickets may take up to 24 hours.

Tag No.: SUP-4.2 How are tickets prioritized?

Tickets are prioritized based on the severity of the issue. Critical system outages are prioritized as Urgent, while general inquiries are handled on a standard timeline.

Tag No.: SUP-5.1 What should I include in a technical issue ticket?

Include a clear description of the issue, any error messages, steps taken to troubleshoot, and screenshots if applicable.

Tag No.: SUP-5.2 What if my issue isn’t resolved with the initial solution provided?

Reply to your ticket and let the support team know. They’ll continue working with you to find a solution.

Tag No.: SUP-6.1 Can I update my account information through a support ticket?

Yes, you can request updates to your account information, such as email changes, password resets, or permission adjustments.

Tag No.: SUP-6.2 How do I reset my password?

Use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page. If you need further assistance, submit a ticket under “Account Support.”

Tag No.: SUP-7.1 Can I suggest a feature for future releases?

Yes! Submit a ticket under “Feature Request,” and include as many details as possible. Our team reviews suggestions regularly.

Tag No.: SUP-8.1 How do I close a ticket once my issue is resolved?

After your issue is resolved, the support team will close the ticket. If it remains open, you can log in to the support portal and close it manually.

Tag No.: SUP-8.2 Can I reopen a ticket if the issue isn’t fully resolved?

Yes, reply to the ticket, and it will be reopened for further assistance.

Tag No.: SUP-9.1 Who should I contact if my ticket hasn’t received a response?

If you haven't received a response within the expected timeframe, contact the Support Lead listed in the support team section.

Tag No.: SUP-9.2 Are there any resources available for troubleshooting common issues?

Yes, please refer to our troubleshooting guide and FAQs section for self-help resources before submitting a ticket.
